Nuisance offers New Yorkers a way to help each other by reporting on obstacles we all want to avoid.

Whether it's slow walkers or subway rats New Yorkers already have enough things to worry about. Nuisance can't do anything about either of those obstacles but it does help you avoid fixed obstacles like: black ice, slumps of garbage, construction sites and even catcallers.
