welcome to Nuisance


Nuisance offers New Yorkers a way to help each other by reporting on obstacles we all want to avoid.

Whether it's noisy parties or bad restaurants which give you food viruses, New Yorkers already have enough things to worry about. Nuisance will help people to avoid such areas where threats to your welfare are present

This is done using NYC Open Data, Google Maps api and IBM Bluemix's Natural Language Classifier
